Bat Ball Stump Game

Welcome to Bat, Ball, Stump Game—an exciting and interactive game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript! In this game, you get to choose between a bat, ball, or stump, while the computer also makes a random choice. The rules are simple: if the computer chooses the bat and you choose the ball, the computer wins; if you choose the bat and the computer chooses the stump, the computer wins. If both you and the computer choose the stump, it's a tie. Test your luck and strategy in this fun game, and see if you can outsmart the computer!

My WittyHacks Hackathon Experience

Participating in the WittyHacks Hackathon was an incredible experience. Our team developed an app called "RoomFinder" to help students find rental rooms easily. The app included features like room listings, filters for amenities, and a contact system for connecting with landlords. We used HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase for our project.


Highlights:

  • Team Formation:

    • I collaborated with a UI designer, a back-end developer, and a front-end developer.

  • Development:

    • We divided tasks based on our skills and worked seamlessly to integrate the front-end with the back-end.

  • Challenges:

    • We encountered issues with real-time data synchronization, which we overcame through teamwork and persistence.

  • Presentation:

    • Our demo went smoothly, and the judges appreciated the app's user-friendly interface and practical utility.


Key Learnings:

Effective collaboration and communication Time management under tight deadlines.
Practical experience with technology integration and problem-solving.
